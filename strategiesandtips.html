Overcoming Anxiety



Activating your calming "Parasympathetic" response

"..When you are controlled by panic you are run by your Negative Observer voice: "I can't..." ("I can't feel this way." "I can't get anxious, because someone will notice." "I can't handle this experience.") As you begin to gain control over panic, you will notice that your voice shifts to that of the Supportive Observer: "It's OK...I can..." ("It's OK to feel this way." "I can be anxious and still perform my job." "I can mange these symptoms.")" Using paradox, you progress to the opposite end of the continuum. You take full responsibility for your symptoms by inviting them.." (Step 6)
