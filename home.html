Introduction

Tech Maverick is a site built to help people develop technological interdependence. In today's day-in-age we are virtually tethered to our devices, as we are captivated by its presence.

My vision was to create an all-inclusive toolkit so that anyone may live a more fulfilling life if they choose so. Technology and mankind should always be a symbiotic realtionship. So that, as a society, each and every one of us has the opportunity to reach his/her full potential.
Let's move forward and leave a lasting impact on society, fulfilling our purpose.

better than waiting to turn gangrene from grieving, I guess.

He is my rest and nutrition,

I am no longer in sin.

A lucky charm to all clovers,

Sprouting what He protracted;

Always reminiscent of the light we see.
